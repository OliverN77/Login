Recommendations Cheap travels

Hacienda Napoles

Hacienda Nápoles, now known as Hacienda Nápoles Theme Park, is a family entertainment center in Puerto Triunfo, Antioquia, Colombia.

Piedra del peñol

El Peñol is a monolithic formation located at the town and municipality of Guatapé, Antioquia in Colombia. A viewing spot has been constructed on top of the rock.

Parque Arvi

Parque Arvi is a natural park located in the northeastern region of Medellín, Colombia. It is a part of the Metropolitan Area of Medellín.
